Greek vs Turkish Backlava

Greek

Greek Backlava usually has walnuts within it and has a filling with usually honey.

Turkish

Turkish Backlava typically contains pistachios within it and has water, suguar, and lemon juice to be used as the filling.

Generic way to make baklava

  1. Lay layers of phyllo dough in a pan
  2. Butter between each layer
  3. Add in desired nut filling
  4. Finish layering and cut a design on the top
  5. Bake the baklava
  6. Add syrup of choice when finished baking
